== Description ==

After you have launched your community, it is essential to make your community easily accessible for your audience. Along with your mobile app, Memeni gives you the ability to add a widget to your website that provides 1-click access to your community. Adding the community widget will help make your website more social and
increase time spent on your site!

Don’t have a community? Click [here](http://memeni.com/landing/wordpress-plugin/?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=ppc&utm_campaign=double-community-audience) to get started.

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Memeni screen, in the left menu, to configure the plugin
4. Fill in your widget ID provided to from the Memeni Community Settings page
5. Choose your desired color.
6. Enable the plugin.
7. Save settings.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I get my widget ID? =

You can get your widget ID in the admin portal of your community. In the Client Website Integration section, copy and paste the value following the ‘cid’ identifier. This is your widget ID. See screenshot 1 for more detail.

= How do I create a community? =
You can create a community by going to memeni.com. Our team will be happy to help you get started on your community.

= Can I customize the text on my widget? =
Yes! Contact your community success manager or head to memeni.com to get in touch with one of our team members and they can help you with customizations.
